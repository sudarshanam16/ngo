Our Mission

To establish an IT park run by disabled people.

To offer opportunities to materialize their different skills.

Guide and train them through specialized training.

Our Aim

 is to Serve the disabled by -

Creating a balance in the society.
Channelizing their skills.
Giving them a platform to showcase their skills.
Organizing events for them.
Building their portal.
Training and polishing them for the future.
Empowering them via employment.
